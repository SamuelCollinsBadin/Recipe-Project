Chard buttered fish

ingredeants

  • butter (1 stick)
  • 1 Cod fish
  • pepper
  • basil

    • Steps

    1. Take cod fish
    2. Cut cod fish into 2 large pieces
    3. Cook cod fish in a mictowave for 30 minutes on max heat
    4. layer one stick of butter on fish
    5. put 20 pinches of pepper on top of fish
    6. add a pinch of basil
    7. attempt to enjoy
