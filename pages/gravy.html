Grandmas gravy

ingrediants

  • Chicken AND beef stock cubes (aka bouillon cubes)
  • Hot water
  • Flour
  • Onion or garlic powder

Steps

  1. Dissolve stock cubes or powder in boiling water;
  2. Melt butter in saucepan and mix in flour;
  3. Pour in stock water while whisking and cook 1.5 minutescod until thickened. DONE!
